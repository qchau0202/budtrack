## Budtrack
A simple budget tracking app, track spending and visualize your financial habits with intuitive, real-time insights

# Highlighted functionalities
- Smart OCR Scanning: Use your device’s camera to scan receipts and bills. The app automatically extracts key details, like amount, date, and vendor, so you don't have to enter them manually.
- Location-Tagged Expenses: Easily pin your expenses to specific locations. This helps you categorize spending by store or venue and provides a clear map of where your money is going.
- Category-Based Budgeting: Set and monitor spending limits for specific categories. The app tracks your progress in real-time, helping you stay within your financial goals for dining, travel, or groceries.
